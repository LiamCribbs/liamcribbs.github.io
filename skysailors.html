Description

Sky Sailors is a physics-based building exploration game. Build modular airships and explore floating islands and alien ruins, unlocking new ship techs as you go.

The game takes place in a procedurally generated world of floating islands and rocks. Players fly between islands, harvesting resources to build up their ship. As they explore, players will discover new plants, creatures, and alien technology.
